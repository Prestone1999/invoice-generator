Help & Guide

Getting Started

Welcome to InvoicePro! Follow these simple steps to create professional invoices:

  1. Fill in your business information
  2. Add client details
  3. Create invoice items with descriptions and prices
  4. Set invoice and due dates
  5. Generate your invoice

Form Fields

  • Company Info: Your business details
  • Client Info: Customer information
  • Items: Products or services being billed
  • Signature: Digital signature for authenticity

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Tab - Navigate between fields
  • Enter - Submit forms
  • Esc - Close dialogs

Pro Tips

  • Use descriptive item names for clarity
  • Set appropriate due dates (usually 30 days)
  • Include payment terms in notes
  • Save invoices as PDF for records
